01637-0489 — Set of 48 Tubes

A combination of price and performance makes Blick Studio Acrylic Colors a great value for students and beginning artists. They have a buttery, oil paint-like texture and are available in several set combinations.

Set of 48 —

This set contains 21 ml (0.7 oz) tubes of 48 colors, including one each of Titanium White, Mars Black, Cadmium Red Medium Hue, Cadmium Yellow Medium Hue, Ultramarine Blue, Ivory Black, Burnt Umber, Cerulean Blue Hue, Cadmium Yellow Light Hue, Burnt Sienna, Alizarin Crimson, Cadmium Orange Hue, Primary Yellow, Cadmium Red Deep Hue, Hooker's Green, Phthalo Blue, Cobalt Blue Hue, Dioxazine Purple, Green Light Permanent, Primary Blue, Gold, Yellow Oxide, Phthalo Green, Bright Aqua Green, Raw Sienna, Deep Violet, Quinacridone Magenta, Silver, Primary Red, Cadmium Red Light Hue, Light Blue Permanent, Brilliant Yellow Green, Titanium Buff, Cadmium Yellow Deep Hue, Brilliant Purple, Raw Umber, Medium Magenta, Neutral Gray, Green Deep Permanent, Light Blue Violet, Light Portrait Pink, Naples Yellow, Copper, Red Oxide, Prism Violet, Bronze, Payne's Gray, and Sap Green.
